§ 02 · On Method Non-partisan · Ad-free · Owner-operated

We don't make the news. We organize it into something you can actually read.

Principle ISources over summaries.

Every number in Ballotics is traceable to a primary source — a polling firm, a federal filing, an election office. You see who said what, when, and by how much.

Principle IINo lectures. No takes.

Ballotics shows you the data and trusts you to read it. No editorial, no opinion columns, no partisan framing — just the instruments professionals use, in a form you can hold.

Principle IIIBuilt to last.

A cycle is four years. An app should be, too. Ballotics is actively developed by one person and designed to outlive any single election — because American politics doesn't take off-years.
